Dust Bowl: An Interactive Game

Created for Writing on the Web for my Master degree in Technical Writing. The topic was chosen by each student, and since Oklahoma was going through a drought period that summer (2022), I was curious to the events during the Dust Bowl.

By using Twine, an open source software used to create storyboards, I recreated different storylines to give users a sense of what life was like before, during, and after the Dust Bowl

Given the time constraints and the magnitude of the available information, I was not able to build the project out to its full potential.
